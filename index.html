With the proper premade starting ingredients, soba can be really easy to make （＞０＜）

Prep time = 5 min

Cook time = 10 min

Total time = 15 min

Ingredients

- 14oz soba noodles

- 1 part Mentsuyu

- 1 part water

Mentsuyu

- 1/4 cup sake

- 1/2 cup soy sauce

- 1/2 cup mirin

- 1 kombu (1" x 1")

- 1 cup katsuobushi (packed)

Toppings/Garnish

- shredded seaweed

- 2 green onions/scallions

- wasabi
