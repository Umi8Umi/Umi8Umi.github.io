With the proper premade starting ingredients, soba can be really easy to make （＞０＜）

Prep time = 5 min

Cook time = 10 min

Total time = 15 min

Ingredients

- 14oz soba noodles

- 1 part Mentsuyu

- 1 part water

Mentsuyu

- 1/4 cup sake

- 1/2 cup soy sauce

- 1/2 cup mirin

- 1 kombu (1" x 1")

- 1 cup katsuobushi (packed)

Toppings/Garnish

- shredded seaweed

- 2 green onions/scallions

- wasabi

Instructions

Dipping Sauce:

1. Add 1/4 cup sake to a medium saucepan and bring it to a boil over meduim high heat. Let the alcohol evaporate for a few seconds

2. Add 1/2 cup soy sauce and 1/2 cup + 1 Tbsp. mirin

3. Add 1 x 1 inch kombu and 1 cup dried bonito flakes

4. Bring it to boil and cook on low heat for 5 minutes. Turn off the heat and set aside.

Boil Soba Noodles:

1. Boil water in a large pot. Add the dried soba noodles to the pot. Seperate the noodles from each other to form a circle around the pot. Stir the noodles on occasion so they don't stick together. Cook to the length indicated on the pckage, each can be slightly different. Be careful not to overcook!

2. Drain the soba noodles into the sieve and rinse the noodles to get rid of the starch under runnign cold water.

3. Transfer the noodles to iced water in a large bowl. Set aside until the noodles are cool.

4. Serve your noodles on a bamboo sieve or mat over a plate (to catch water from the noodles). Put the garnish on top.

5. Put one part of cooled dipping sauce and 2-3 parts of iced water in a serving pitcher or small individual bowls. Put chopped green onions and wasabi on a small plate and serve with the soba noodles.

ENJOY!
